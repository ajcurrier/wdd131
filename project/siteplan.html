Pedal Cycle Provo Site Plan

Site Name:

Pedal Cycle Provo

This name represents the site's goal to share the essential information for picking up pedal cycling (aka bicycling) in Provo, Utah

pedalcycleprovo.com

Site Purpose:

This site is meant to introduce guests to the local laws and safety precautions that they need to safely and legally pedal cycle in Provo. We will also include tips on the best trails

Scenarios:

Is Provo bicycle-friendly? Bicycle commuter friendly?

Am I legally required to install lights on my bike?

Can I ride on Sidewalks?

Is Provo a safe place to pedal cycle?

Are there designated bicycle lanes?

Are there strategically placed locations where I can secure my bike?

Are there scenic locations where I can pedal cycle for leisure?

What about locations for mountain biking?

Am I legally required to wear a helmet?

How big of an issue is bicycle theft in Provo? What kind of security measures do I need to take to protect my bike from theft?
